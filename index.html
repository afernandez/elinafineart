  • Hi, my name is Elina Arrom and I'm passionate about painting artwork that has meaning and expresses sentiments that everyone can related to.

    I was born in Cuba and studied fine art in a professional academy for ten years. My style comes from a blend of the elegance of romanticism of the Old World combined with the freshness and surrealism of the New. One of the distinguishing qualities of my work is my attention to detail that emphasizes nature and the human form.

    Over the past three decades, I've shown my work in over thirty personal, private, and collective exhibitions showcasing both paintings and sculptures. Some of my pieces are representative of my roots and reflect Cubanostalgia.

    I specialize in acrylic and oil paintings that range from as small as 6'' x 6'' to as big as 48'' x 64''. Several of my collections are inspired by the Renaissance, nature, symbolism, and even stories and books.
    Mannequins: This collection symbolizes the life of inanimate objects during the Renaissance.
    Cubanostalgia: Deep and close to my heart is Cuba, and the folklore of Cubanostalgia.
    Little Prince: This is one of my absolute favorite books and has a special meaning as a child, an adult, and later in life. I've encompassed symbolism from Antoinne de Saint-Exupery's book, such as the fox, caterpillar, rose, and more in several of my paintings.


    From an Italian garden for reflection, to a haven for daydreaming, wall murals can transform a room into whatever you want it to be. Murals are fun to achieve, will enhance your walls, and can instantly change a room from ordinary to breathaking!

    If you're interested in stage design for special events such as weddings, sweet 16s and quinceañeras, then I can design 3d pieces, backdrops, and other elements.
    For example, I've done a horse-drawn carriage for a sweet 16, italian murals for landscapes and theater plays, movie characters, etc.


    The best way to appreciate art is to actually try it. I've taught art for many years to both elementary school students and even the elderly, and they never cease to amaze me with their creations. My classes include all of the following,
    drawing: perspective, human figure, animals, still live
    painting: water colors, acrylics, and oils
    sculpture: pottery, figurines
